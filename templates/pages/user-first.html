An encryption software?

Security solutions should be easy to use and compliment your workflows. Good news: you can now embrace your favorite cloud services at work, Tanker makes sure that all your data stored and exchanged is 100% business compliant.

No additional process or complex installation, Tanker app seamlessly ciphers data locally on your devices before it’s transported and stored on Dropbox or Microsoft servers.

Only the people you have chosen to collaborate with will access your files upon invitation, whether they are internal or external collaborators. Others will just see a long, meaningless string of numbers.

Easy setup

1. Download Tanker for your OS

2. Pair your cloud account to your Tanker application

3. Generate your unique encryption key, start securing your data in seconds

Encrypt as you go

Work and stock your files directly in your Tanker drive.
Tanker works behind the scenes to encrypt your files before they leave your computer.

Collaborate securely

Keep sharing files the same way you did before.
- Seamlessly with your team members
- Upon simple free invitation with external partners

Tanker does not require any expertise nor infrastructure; our pure software solution is open to everyone. Recipients outside your company circle can simply install the Tanker app in one click, without the need of admin rights.

Frequently asked...

Am I supposed to download Dropbox or OneDrive?

Yes, you always need to have Dropbox or OneDrive desktop client setup on your computer. Tanker’s know-how is to provide world-class encryption on your data. We let our cloud partners manage what they are the best at: stock, sync and share.

What if the person I want to share a document with does not have Tanker?

Tanker’s infrastructure enables you to share documents inside and outside your company with full end-to-end encryption.  
If you share a collaborative folder, the recipient will have to setup Tanker on his computer in order to decrypt the documents. Installing Tanker only takes a few seconds so your collaborator will be up and running in no time.

What if I forget my PIN code or lose my device?

If you lose your PIN Code, your unique encryption key will be cancelled and you will have to click “Reset PIN Code” to get back access to your data. Your administrator (someone in your IT team) will make sure you can access it back.
